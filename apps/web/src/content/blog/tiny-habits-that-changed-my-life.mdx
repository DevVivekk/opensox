tiny health habits that changed my life

- not eating anything three hrs before going to bed
- drinking two glasses of water after waking up in the morning
- sleeping 7-8 hrs/day
- sleeping at sharp 11pm
- limiting junk only once a month
- moving out of delhi
- eating 2-3 raw amla (as achar) per day (high in antioxidants)
- min 70 gms protein per day
- fasting on some festivals like janmastami, mahashivratri
- working out (default for me)
- not eating meat (am prone to allergy)
- coffee once a day
- wearing a masks when on road (dust)
- also, i keep learning about health and nutrition and keep doing some experiments that bring more insights.
- always using cold water for bathing. (irrespective of the weather)
