Many people don't realise that contributing to open source has asymmetric results.

Most of us think, it's linear (for a job/internship only) but it's not.

Open source not only sharpens your skills, but also:

- Improve communication
- Teach how to maintain a codebase
- Best practices to build a software
- Make connections
- Make you visible

And a lot.

Just make contributions and see all of this happening.
